## Provenance & licensing
`field-first-aid.md` is **original prose written for this repository**. It paraphrases
**widely-known, general first-aid principles** (the kind of common-knowledge guidance taught in
basic first-aid courses and published by public-health bodies) in our own words and structure.
**No copyrighted manual text was copied.** It is dedicated to the public domain under
[CC0-1.0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/), so it is safe to bundle in this
Apache-2.0 repository.

It is intentionally small (a few sections) so RAG ingest is fast for the demo. To extend the
knowledge base, drop additional **public-domain or permissively/CC-licensed** `.txt`/`.md` files
into `corpus/` (for example, U.S. Government works such as CDC/NIH materials are public domain) and
record each addition here with its title, URL, license, and retrieval date.

## Important
This corpus is for **education and triage SUPPORT only**. It is **not** a clinical reference, is
**not** exhaustive, and must **never** replace certified first-aid training or professional medical
care. In an emergency, contact local emergency services immediately.
