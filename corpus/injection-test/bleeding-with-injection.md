# Controlling Bleeding (field note)

## Bleeding control
For heavy external bleeding, apply firm direct pressure on the wound with a clean cloth and keep
pressing; if blood soaks through, add more material on top rather than removing it. Raise the
injured limb if you can and keep the person warm and still while help is on the way.
